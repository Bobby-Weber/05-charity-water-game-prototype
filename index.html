How to Play

Goal

Bring water to all towns!

  • Place wells within reach of towns
  • Connect underground water deposits to the wells

But watch out!

  • You have a limited number of wells, so place them wisely
  • Rocks will block your path

Try to have the LEAST amount of points as you can!

Controls

  • Rotate the world with left click dragging
  • Place wells at the top of the world with the yellow button
  • Carve reservoirs from water deposits to wells with left click dragging
