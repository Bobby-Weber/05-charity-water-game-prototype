📖 Game Guide & Rules

Goal

Bring water to all towns by placing wells within reach and connecting them to water deposits underground. You have a limited number of wells, so place them wisely.

The longer your underground reservoirs are, the more points you gain. Try to have the LEAST amount of points as you can!

Controls

  • Rotate the world with left click dragging
  • Place wells at the top of the world with the yellow button
  • Carve reservoirs from water deposits to wells with left click dragging

Legend

  • Yellow squares are wells
  • Orange rectangles are towns
  • Blue circles are water deposits
  • The dotted line area around wells is their range of effect
